About Us

We are a small team committed to helping rescue animals find loving homes. Every adoption supports medical care and food for more pets in need.At Paws & Love, we believe every animal deserves a second chance at happiness. Our mission is to create a safe and loving environment for pets until they find their forever homes. With a passionate team of volunteers, veterinarians, and caregivers, we ensure that each pet receives proper medical care, food, and love.
